Flow overview

How Xstellar qualifies every DM.

A simple, opinionated pipeline that takes a raw Instagram message and turns it into a fully qualified lead for your team.

Step 01

A new DM hits your inbox

Every inbound message on your Instagram account is captured instantly, even outside business hours.

Step 02

Xstellar automation fires

A secure webhook forwards the conversation into your Xstellar pipeline the moment the DM is received.

Step 03

Claude runs qualification

Claude asks clarifying questions, understands intent, and scores the lead based on your ideal customer profile.

Step 04

The lead lands in Airtable

Qualified leads are written to Airtable with score, context, transcript, and structured fields ready for your CRM.

Step 05

Your team gets pinged

High-intent leads are pushed straight into Slack so your team can follow up while the conversation is still warm.
