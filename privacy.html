Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to XStellar Systems ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate xstellar.systems and provide AI-powered Instagram direct message lead qualification services (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register for our Service, we collect your name, email address, business name, and payment information.
  • Instagram Business Account Data: When you connect your Instagram Business Account, we collect your Instagram User ID, access tokens, and business account information.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Instagram Direct Messages: We collect and process direct messages sent to your connected Instagram Business Account for the purpose of lead qualification and scoring.
  • Message Metadata: Including sender information, message timestamps, and engagement data.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Service, including log data, device information, and analytics.

2.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Meta/Instagram: We receive data from Meta's Instagram platform through their official APIs, including message content, user profiles (to the extent permitted by Meta), and engagement metrics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain the Service: Process Instagram direct messages, qualify leads using AI analysis, and deliver scored results to you.
  • Improve Our Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our AI models and Service functionality.
  • Communication: Send you service-related notifications, updates, and support responses.
  • Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Business Operations: Process payments, prevent fraud, and maintain security.

4. AI Processing and Lead Scoring

Our Service uses artificial intelligence (specifically, Claude AI by Anthropic) to:

  • Analyze incoming Instagram direct messages
  • Score leads based on relevance and quality (A/B/C classification)
  • Extract key information from messages

Data sent to AI processors: Message content and metadata are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not use it to train AI models.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • AI Processing: Anthropic (Claude API) for message analysis
  • Data Storage: Airtable for lead database management
  • Notifications: Slack for real-time notifications
  • Payment Processing: Payment processors for subscription billing
  • Hosting Providers: Cloud infrastructure providers for service hosting

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

6. Data Retention

  • Instagram Messages: We retain processed messages for 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted unless required for legal compliance.
  • Lead Data: Stored in your Airtable workspace indefinitely or until you request deletion.
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your subscription and for 1 year after account closure for legal and accounting purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure storage of access tokens and credentials
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
