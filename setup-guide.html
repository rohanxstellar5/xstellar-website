Switch to Business Account

Instagram must be a Business or Creator account

Already have a Business account? Skip to the next step. You can check in Instagram Settings → Account → Account type.
  1. 1
    Open Instagram App

    Go to your profile and tap the menu (☰) in the top right

  2. 2
    Go to Settings

    Tap Settings and privacy → Account → Switch account type

    Settings → Account → Switch account type
  3. 3
    Select Business or Creator

    Choose "Switch to professional account" and select Business (recommended) or Creator

  4. 4
    Complete the Setup

    Select your business category and complete the profile. You can skip connecting to Facebook for now.

Don't see "Switch account type"? Your account might already be a Business account. Check if you see "Professional dashboard" on your profile.
Instagram Help Guide

Create a Facebook Page

You need a Facebook Page to receive Instagram DMs via our system

Already have a Facebook Page? Skip to the next step. Make sure you're an Admin of the Page.
  1. 1
    Go to Facebook Pages

    Click the button below to open Facebook Page creation

  2. 2
    Enter Your Page Name

    Use your business name or brand name. This can be the same as your Instagram username.

  3. 3
    Select a Category

    Choose the category that best describes your business (e.g., "Marketing Agency", "Consulting", "Local Business")

  4. 4
    Create the Page

    Click "Create Page". You can skip adding profile picture and other details for now.

Create Facebook Page
Important: You must be logged into Facebook with a personal account to create a Page. The Page will be managed by your personal account.

Connect Page to Instagram

Link your Facebook Page to your Instagram Business account

Already connected? If you connected your Page during Instagram Business setup, skip to the final step.

Option A: Connect from Instagram (Recommended)

  1. 1
    Open Instagram Settings

    Go to your profile → Menu (☰) → Settings and privacy

  2. 2
    Go to Account Settings

    Tap "Account" → "Sharing to other apps" (or "Linked accounts")

    Settings → Account → Sharing to other apps
  3. 3
    Connect to Facebook

    Tap "Facebook" and log in to your Facebook account

  4. 4
    Select Your Page

    Choose the Facebook Page you want to connect to this Instagram account

Option B: Connect from Facebook

  1. 1
    Open Your Facebook Page

    Go to your Facebook Page and click "Settings" (or the gear icon)

  2. 2
    Find Instagram Settings

    In the left menu, click "Linked accounts" or search for "Instagram"

    Page Settings → Linked accounts → Instagram
  3. 3
    Connect Instagram Account

    Click "Connect account" and log in to your Instagram Business account

Connection not working? Make sure you're using the same Facebook account that owns the Page, and that your Instagram is a Business/Creator account.

Ready to Connect!

Your Instagram is set up correctly. Let's connect it to Xstellar.

Quick Checklist — Make sure you have:
  • ✓
    Instagram Business or Creator account
  • ✓
    A Facebook Page (you're an Admin)
  • ✓
    Facebook Page connected to Instagram

When you click "Connect Instagram", you'll be asked to:

  1. Log in to Facebook (if not already)
  2. Select which Facebook Pages to share
  3. Grant Xstellar permission to read your Instagram DMs
Privacy Note: Xstellar only reads messages that contain your trigger word. We never read or store any other conversations.
